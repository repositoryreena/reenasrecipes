Deviled Eggs

I made deviled eggs for the Easter party, and honestly... I’m still not sure how they turned out. People *looked* at them, a few even took some, but there wasn’t a lot of feedback. By the end of the night, they’d been sitting out a little too long and definitely crossed into questionable territory. Lesson learned: either chill them properly or make something less perishable next time!
