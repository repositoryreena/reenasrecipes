Baked Mac and Cheese

My baked mac and cheese was a total hit this year, and I know it’s going to become a holiday staple from now on. The creamy cheese sauce, perfectly baked until golden and bubbly, had everyone coming back for seconds (and thirds!). I used a combination of sharp cheddar and creamy mozzarella to give it the perfect balance of flavor and richness. The crispy cheese topping added that irresistible crunch, making every bite absolutely delicious. It’s officially on the menu every year—no holiday is complete without it!

Sweet Potato Casserole

I tried making sweet potato casserole this Thanksgiving, and honestly… the only part I really liked was the marshmallows on top. The toasted gooey layer was perfection!

I definitely put in way too much effort peeling and cutting the raw sweet potatoes when I could have just boiled them first—lesson learned.

If I ever make it again, I’m giving it a makeover and using pink marshmallows to make it extra cute and totally my style.
