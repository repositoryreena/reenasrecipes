Baked Mac and Cheese

My baked mac and cheese was a total hit this year, and I know it’s going to become a holiday staple from now on. The creamy cheese sauce, perfectly baked until golden and bubbly, had everyone coming back for seconds (and thirds!). I used a combination of sharp cheddar and creamy mozzarella to give it the perfect balance of flavor and richness. The crispy breadcrumb topping added that irresistible crunch, making every bite absolutely delicious. It’s officially on the menu every year—no holiday is complete without it!
