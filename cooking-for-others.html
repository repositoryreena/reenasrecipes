Black and White Cookies for the Choir

For a choir picnic, I needed to bring something sweet for 15 people—so when I saw a box of exactly 15 black and white cookies, it felt like fate.

I chose them not just because they were convenient, but because they reminded me of piano keys—black and white, together in harmony—just like our voices used to be.

They were a hit, and more than a few of us smiled at the little symbolism. Music brought us together, and that day, so did cookies.
