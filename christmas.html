Christmas Cheese Board

This Christmas Tree Cheese Board was inspired by an idea I found while scrolling through Pinterest—and honestly, it turned out even cuter than I imagined. I wasn’t sure I’d have all the right ingredients, but luckily my mother had everything we needed tucked away in the fridge and pantry. We layered cheese and crackers into a tree shape, complete with a little row of crackers for the trunk and star-shaped crackers for the top. We even added a ribbon for decoration. It was festive, fun, and totally delicious—definitely a new holiday tradition!
