Wonder Woman Apron – Apron Atelier Designs

For my mom’s birthday, I wanted to get her something personal—something that said *“you’re a hero.”*

I found the perfect gift on Etsy: a Wonder Woman apron from the shop Apron Atelier Designs. It was colorful, bold, and strong—just like her.

She wore it with pride the next time she cooked, and I couldn’t stop smiling. She really is my Wonder Woman, in and out of the kitchen.
